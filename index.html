Service: Local technical support preferred

Language: Arabic interface and documentation preferred

Major Airports & Seaports of Egypt

Major Airports

  • Cairo International Airport (CAI) - Egypt's primary international gateway with 3 terminals
  • Hurghada International Airport (HRG) - Major tourist destination on Red Sea
  • Luxor International Airport (LXR) - Gateway to ancient Egyptian monuments
  • Sharm El Sheikh International Airport (SSH) - Popular resort destination airport
  • Alexandria International Airport (HBE) - Serving Egypt's second largest city

Major Seaports

  • Port of Alexandria - Egypt's main commercial port handling 60% of foreign trade
  • Port Said - Strategic location at Mediterranean entrance to Suez Canal
  • Port of Damietta - Important container and Ro-Ro terminal
  • Port of Suez - Key port at southern entrance to Suez Canal
  • Ain Sokhna Port - Industrial port on Gulf of Suez with direct canal access
